About

Internet Software compagny

We are Internet Software compagny located in Toulouse, France specialist in the design, development of interactive web experiences.

We are built from the ground up using edge technology. Over the past year I've shifted my focus towards Responsive Web Design or RWD. So, why Responsive Web Design? Well to help you better visualize the importance of RWD all you really need to see are the numbers. The following shows you the percent mobile traffic accounts for based off all web traffic in the World from the past 3 years. In Q1 of 2011 mobile web traffic only accounted for 4.5%. A year later you see that percentage jump from 4.5% to 13.14% in Q1 of 2012. In Q1 of 2013 mobile traffic continues to grow (23.86%) and analysts are predicting that mobile traffic will overtake desktop traffic in 2014.

What We Do, What We Use, and How We Do It

We live in a connected World that's full of exciting new products and services, and with that the adoption rate of devices has quickly become the fastest growing consumer-driven technology of all-time. With the ever changing mobile landscape it's critical for your website to be fluid, and have the ability to adapt to whatever device the viewer's using.

This process is called Responsive Web Design or RWD and, coincidently, this is where I come in. Instead of me talk about, what may sound like a foreign language, common RWD practices such as break points, frameworks, and media queries I'm just going to show you.

Below are two screenshots of everyone's favorite, newegg. The image on your left is your basic, static, web design. That doesn't look too easy to navigate from a phone if you were to ask me. The screenshot to the left is newegg's mobile website. These are actually two different website's, which is frowned upon by Google, but regardless you're provided a better experience if you're having to use your phone.

What I strive to do is build websites that provide your customers' the best possible user experience when they're viewing your website ‐ whether they're using a 3.5" smartphone or a 30" display. I'm not just building you a website, but also providing you a value-added service to help attract and retain new customers by giving them a enjoyable experience while viewing your website.
